  Valley TV show) became public meme.

  before noon

  replaced with albumin protein to clear toxins

  cognition, sleep, etc.

### FDA position

The FDA has officially warned against "young blood"
infusions — no proven clinical benefit, potentially
harmful. Johnson discontinued that specific protocol
after his own measurements showed no benefit.
