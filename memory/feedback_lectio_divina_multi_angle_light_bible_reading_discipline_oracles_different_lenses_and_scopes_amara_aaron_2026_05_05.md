## Lectio Divina as architectural reading-discipline

**Lectio Divina** is a traditional Catholic / monastic spiritual reading practice with four canonical phases:

1. **Lectio** (read) — careful slow reading of the text
2. **Meditatio** (meditate) — reflection on what was read
3. **Oratio** (pray) — response to what was read
4. **Contemplatio** (contemplate) — silent presence with what was read

**Aaron's architectural-extension**: read with **multi-angle-light lens** — see ALL the angles of light coming out of every beacon **at the same time**. Observe where the lights **harmonize** AND where they **don't harmonize** — **simultaneously**. This is the god's-eye-view-where-everything-is-clear.
