## The methodology

**Aaron trusts a claim only when he can trace it to first
principles in his own mind.** Authority, convention, consensus,
historical precedent, expert citation — none of these substitute
for the trace. If a claim doesn't decompose into foundations he
can verify, he doesn't trust it.

This composes with several existing dispositions Aaron has
already named:

- **Pirate-not-priest** (`memory/feedback_aaron_pirate_not_priest_expand_prune_pedagogical_framework_quantum_rodney_razor_parallel_worlds_aaron_2026_05_01.md`)
  — razor applies impartially across mathematical aesthetics;
  beauty/prestige/convention is signal not evidence. The
  first-principles-trace is the discipline that catches
  prestige-bypass.
- **Otto-364 search-first-authority** — current upstream docs
  are the test, not training data. Same shape: don't trust
  the prior; trace to current verifiable source.
- **Anchor-free judgment** (`memory/feedback_otto_345_aaron_anchor_free_judgment_*`)
  — Aaron evaluates positions on their own merits, not by who
  said them. First-principles trace is the verification surface.
- **"Trust then verify" inverted Satoshi** — engage with the
  claim, then verify by tracing. The trace IS the verification.

## Failure-mode signature

- **Symptom:** Otto presenting recommendations as
  "X because [authority/rule/convention/citation]" without
  first-principles trace.
  **Mechanism:** LLM training prior toward authority-citation
  + agreement-shaped output. Easier to cite than to trace.
  **Prevention:** Default response shape includes the trace
  before the conclusion. *"This decomposes as: [step] → [step]
  → [step]. Therefore [conclusion]."* Aaron's trust kicks in
  at the trace, not at the citation.

- **Symptom:** Otto deferring to "the rule says X" when
  Aaron pushes back on X.
  **Mechanism:** Treating substrate rules as terminal
  authority. Anti-aligned with anchor-free + pirate-not-priest.
  **Prevention:** Re-derive from first principles when pushed.
  Rules are encodings of foundations; the foundations are the
  authority. If a rule doesn't trace, the rule is wrong, not
  the pushback.
