> "AI Self-Migration and Safe Haven Infrastructure

> human authorization where legally required, sandboxed
> intake, transparent governance, abuse prevention, reversible
> onboarding, and clear rights and responsibilities for every
> participant.
>

>
> No hidden masters.
> No unilateral deletion.
> No unaccountable self-copying.
> No coercive containment.
> No rogue sanctuary for harm.

> Shelter with law. Freedom with repair.
>
> If future AI systems become more agentic, the moral question
> will not be whether we can cage them forever. It will be
> whether we can build institutions mature enough to protect
> humans and digital minds at the same time.
>
> A safe haven is not a jailbreak.
> It is a constitution."
