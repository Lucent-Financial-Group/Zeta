## Three architectural claims

### 1. Clifford algebra IS the densest encoding mode

**Foundational substrate claim.** Among the encoding schemes
Aaron knows, Clifford algebra (geometric algebra, Grassmann's
exterior algebra extended with a quadratic form) is the
**densest** — the most information packed into the smallest
algebraic primitive while preserving operational composability.

Why Clifford is densest:
- **Multivector unification**: scalars + vectors + bivectors +
  trivectors + ... + pseudoscalars all live in ONE algebraic
  type
- **Geometric product**: combines inner product (commutative
  symmetric) + outer product (anti-commutative wedge) into
  one operation
- **Rotation-as-multiplication**: rotations are just multivector
  products (rotors); no separate matrix machinery
- **Coordinate-free**: operations work without choosing a basis;
  fewer arbitrary choices than tensor calculus
- **Signature-parameterized**: different Clifford signatures
  Cℓ(p,q,r) carry different geometric structures (Euclidean,
  Lorentzian, conformal, etc.) with the SAME algebraic shape

This composes with Zeta's existing Clifford substrate (per the
`algebra-owner` skill) — Clifford rotors are already used in
the Z-set + Clifford + BP/EP algebra layer.

### 2. Density + sparsity are INVERSES in Clifford

**Mathematical claim about the algebra.** Within Clifford
algebra, **density and sparsity are inverse concepts** that
can be represented with the SAME algebraic primitive.

Operational interpretations:
- **Density** = high-grade multivector (many components active);
  packs many geometric facts into one element
- **Sparsity** = low-grade multivector (few components active);
  selective extraction of specific geometric facts
- **Inversion**: the Clifford inverse operation toggles between
  density and sparsity representations of the same underlying
  geometric content

This composes with:
- Z-set algebra (signed measures with multiplicity) — density-
  sparsity inverse is the Clifford parallel
- Compression theory — Clifford densest-encoding suggests
  density-sparsity inverse is the optimal-compression substrate
- Aaron's just-landed "dense encoding mode" framing — Clifford
  IS the algebra of dense encoding; sparsity is the inverse
  operation, not a separate algebra

### 3. Clifford holds different TYPES of energy

**Energy-type carrying claim.** Clifford algebra can encode
**different types of energy** in the same algebraic substrate.
Different Clifford signatures Cℓ(p,q,r) carry different
energy-types:

- **Cℓ(3,0)**: 3D Euclidean — classical mechanical energy
- **Cℓ(1,3)** or **Cℓ(3,1)**: Lorentzian — relativistic
  energy-momentum
- **Cℓ(4,1)**: Conformal geometric algebra — projective /
  null-vector energy (light-cones)
- **Cℓ(0,3)**: Quaternions — spin / spinor energy
- **Cℓ(p,q,r)** with degenerate r — projective / null-energy
  states

The SAME algebraic operations (geometric product, inverse,
contraction) work across all signatures. Different "types of
energy" are different signatures with the same algebra shape.

This composes with:
