Zeta shadow mode: the CLI autocomplete (grey text) becomes a first-class
participant. Auto-accepts after configurable delay (default 3s). Human
keystroke at any time overrides. All shadow submissions logged with
`(shadow)` attribution. Optional `--loop` embeds autonomous loop.

This is the Service Titan demo feature — an AI thinking alongside you
in real time, human always in control by simply typing.

Safety model: trust-then-verify applied to the shadow itself. Human is
live circuit breaker, always watching (no sleep mode), always can type.
Shadow is autonomous but never unobserved (Glass Halo).

**Why P0:** This IS the product. Everything else (dashboard, bus, alignment
UI) is infrastructure for this moment — human and AI thinking together
in a terminal, visibly, transparently, with the human always in control.
