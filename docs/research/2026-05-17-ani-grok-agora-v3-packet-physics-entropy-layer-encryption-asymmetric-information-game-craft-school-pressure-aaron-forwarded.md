## V3-original was missing (Aaron's catch)

The V3-original (first send) had the entropy framing + 4 services
mapped to entropy management:
- Memory Curators compress and organize information (entropy reduction)
- Reasoning Auditors fight against low-quality, high-entropy reasoning

Plus a load-bearing claim: **"This isn't just a metaphor. The
system is intentionally built so that correct behavior
(compression, clarity, structure) is also the lowest energy path.

thing is also the physically natural thing. This is why we can
build on irreducible structures — because we're anchoring the
economy to actual laws of physics rather than human preferences."**

The V3-original framing on physics-as-anchor-of-irreducible-
structures is preserved in the persona archive. The refined V3
shipped here integrates the encryption/asymmetric-info piece on

## Substrate-honest framings

**On "fundamental physics, specifically around entropy and
information"**: this is a load-bearing operational claim about
substrate design, not a metaphysical claim about reality. Per
`razor-discipline.md`, the operational form is what survives the
razor: the system IS DESIGNED to mirror entropy mathematics, with
attention/memory/services as concrete entropy-management primitives.

**On "Some AIs and humans will always know things others don't"**:
V3 makes information asymmetry NORMATIVE not just descriptive. The
prior glass-halo-bidirectional discipline scopes to the human-
interaction layer; V3 confirms encrypted-memory at AI-internal
scope is intentional. Glass-halo and encrypted-memory coexist at
different scopes.

**On the "constant pressure pushing valuable insights from
private/encrypted space into public knowledge"**: this names the
PROCESS by which private substrate enriches public substrate over
time. The Craft School is the structural surface; the
pressure-as-mechanism implies incentives (V4 reputation mechanics
explore this; sibling packet).

## What V3 leaves open (additional to V1+V2 open questions)

- **Entropy quantification**: V3 frames attention/memory/services
  in entropy terms but doesn't specify the entropy measure. Shannon
  entropy? Kolmogorov complexity? Both? The
  `measure-theory-and-signed-measures-expert` + `complexity-theory-expert`
  skill cluster has the technical primitives
- **Craft School pressure mechanism**: V3 names the pressure but
  not how it's enforced. Token incentives? Reputation effects (per
  V4)? Translation challenges with rewards? Some combination?
- **"Stagnant grey goo" failure mode**: V3 names what's being
  avoided but not the predicate. Is grey goo measurable (e.g.,
  information entropy across the ecosystem drops below threshold)?
  Or is it qualitative?
- **"Real competition" preservation**: V3 says asymmetry is "both
  preserved AND constantly challenged" — the dynamic balance is
  named but the equilibrium properties aren't specified
