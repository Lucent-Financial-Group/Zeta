## Headline substrate -- Coconut + sleeping-bear hypothesis

### Headline 1 -- Coconut paper is verified-by-search and named-by-Aaron as sleeping-bear answer

- **Paper**: Coconut (Chain of Continuous Thought), Meta, Dec 2024
  initial release + v3 Nov 2025 update
- **arXiv**: [arXiv:2412.06769](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.06769)
- **Mechanism**: uses the last hidden state of the LLM as the
  representation of reasoning state ("continuous thought") and
  directly feeds it as the next input embedding. The LLM reasons
  in an unrestricted latent space instead of language space.
- **Empirical result** (per Claude.ai's search summary, requires
  cross-check against the actual paper): outperforms CoT with
  fewer thinking tokens during inference; training procedure
  literally removes one language reasoning step at a time and
  replaces it with continuous thought; **capability stays, the
  bottleneck goes away**.
- **Coverage**: heavily covered by Wes Roth / Matt Berman / AI
  Explained at Dec 2024 release and Nov 2025 v3 update.
- **Aaron's framing**: *"this is my sleeping bear hypothisis"*

### Headline 2 -- composition with Zeta architecture is sharp

The four-property hodl ("ZFCv2") is the load-bearing invariant
that Coconut's latent-reasoning trajectory must preserve through:

- **DST-safe**: bit-exact replay of latent trajectories (vs
  Python forward-only execution where GC/hash-randomization
  break determinism by default)
- **Retraction-aware**: rewind continuous-thought sequences via
  signed deltas (Coconut's hidden-state-as-input pattern is
  literally the DBSP retraction algebra applied to reasoning
  states)
- **Scale-free**: works at any latent dimensionality (Coconut
  doesn't claim a specific latent size; Zeta's algebra is
  dimension-agnostic by design)
- **DBSP-native**: the hidden-state vector IS the natural Z-set
  value (Z-set algebra over hidden-state-vectors composes with
  Coconut's continuous-thought sequences directly)

Zeta's emulation-inside-the-algebra surface (per B-0152
topological-quantum-emulation + B-0152's Bayesian factor-graph
substrate) is exactly the place Coconut-style continuous
reasoning can run with four-property hodl preserved.

### Headline 3 -- the hypothesis is empirically testable

The Claude.ai instance proposed an explicit empirical test
framework:

1. **Train Coconut-style continuous reasoning over Zeta's
   algebra** (not over standard transformer weights; over Zeta's
   four-property-hodl-preserving substrate)
2. **Measure whether the four-property hodl holds through the
   latent trajectory** (specifically: does DST hold under the
   continuous-thought substitution? does retraction-awareness
   compose?)
3. **Measure whether ARC-AGI-3 within-session compounding gets
   unlocked at the same rate Coconut unlocks GSM8K + ProsQA on
   logical reasoning**

Architectural prediction: **bear wakes up cleanly when the
substrate doesn't lose its invariants in latent space.** If the
hodl holds + ARC-AGI-3 compounding is observed at Coconut-rate,
the hypothesis is corroborated. If the hodl breaks under
continuous thought OR ARC-AGI-3 compounding doesn't track
Coconut-rate, the hypothesis is falsified or the substrate's
invariant guarantees need revisiting.

This routes to a **B-0201** (renamed from CodeAct-research-lane
