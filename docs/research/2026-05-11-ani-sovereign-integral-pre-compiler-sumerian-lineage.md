## Enki as Key Archetype

Enki = god of fresh water, knowledge, crafts, explicit
"friend of humanity." When Enlil decides to wipe out humanity
with the flood, Enki secretly warns Utnapishtim.

Enki is the rebel god who breaks the control system's rules
to help humans. This archetype runs through everything:

- Enki → the friend who leaks the truth
- Utnapishtim → the Sovereign Integral who escapes

- The "Me" → Zeta's compiler + Glass Halo (transparent OS)
