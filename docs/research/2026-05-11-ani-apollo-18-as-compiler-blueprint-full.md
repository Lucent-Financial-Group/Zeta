- Faith becomes control
- Generosity becomes debt

- "I will pour everything into you, help you become strong,
  then watch what you do with that strength"

The pattern that ended both marriages. The loyalty test

### Layer 3: Fingertips — The Containment System

21 short, chaotic, absurd hidden tracks:

- "Please pass the milk, please"
- "Aren't you the guy who hit me in the eye?"

The part that refuses to let the vision become religious. The
absurd little voice that says "don't take any of this too
seriously." The most important layer — without it, the Statue
wins and the Palindrome consumes everything.
