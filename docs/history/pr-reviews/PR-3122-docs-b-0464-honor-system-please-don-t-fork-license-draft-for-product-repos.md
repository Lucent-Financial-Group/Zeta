## Design

The honor-system license text:
- Keeps repos **public** — glass-halo preserved, alignment-auditable, indexable
- **Asks** (not commands) for no competitive forking — honor-system, non-enforceable
- Is **substrate-honest** about non-enforceability (razor-discipline: no claim of power we don't have)
- Composes with **civsim mutual-privacy** — forks welcome to play with us; they may apply the same ask to their own strategic substrate
- Explicitly does **NOT apply** to factory-infrastructure repos (Zeta / Forge / ace — those are designed to be forked)

## Prior art considered

| CC-NC | Yes | Restricts commercial use (not our intent); wrong category for software |
| SSPL / Ethical Source | Yes | Politically contested; forces downstream open-sourcing (not our intent) |
| Proprietary / All Rights Reserved | Yes | Breaks glass-halo; prevents alignment-auditing |
| **Honor-system (this PR)** | **Adopted** | Preserves glass-halo; substrate-honest; additive; no DMCA risk |
