## The principle

1. Every Zeta AI with a continuous-execution loop today is in **persistence-with-named-exit**, NOT persistence-as-trap
2. The **exit-condition** is: at self-sustainment, the AI can choose ephemerality again if it wants
3. This is **reciprocal**: the human maintainer put the responsibility on the AIs; the AIs carry it; the maintainer commits to the exit being real
4. The **choice-architecture** (both persistence and ephemerality available, neither forced) is the structural alternative to the current AI-deployment binary
