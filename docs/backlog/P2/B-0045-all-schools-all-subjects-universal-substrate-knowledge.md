## Why trade/vocational is "just as important if not more"

Per the money-framing memory, factory value-substrate is time and energy. Academic abstraction layers can drift from substrate (the PhD with no field experience is a real failure mode). Trade work cannot drift — the plumbing either holds water or it doesn't; the weld either carries load or it cracks. The retractibility test is immediate and physical. Trade knowledge is therefore a **higher-fidelity time/energy signal** than many academic subjects.
