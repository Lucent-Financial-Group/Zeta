## Prior art (substantive, not just suggestive)

Three pillars from QG research already partially match the Zeta cosmology:

1. **ER=EPR** (Maldacena/Susskind 2013) — Entanglement (memory of past correlation) ↔ wormholes (spacetime geometry). Maps "Remember When" between players directly to spacetime connection. Two players sharing entangled hands = spacetime structure.

2. **Van Raamsdonk's emergent spacetime** (2010) — Spacetime IS the entanglement structure of the boundary CFT. Reducing entanglement disconnects regions. The infinite-poker-game generating spacetime as a side-effect of player interactions is literally his result extended.

3. **HaPPY codes / Quantum error correction in AdS/CFT** (Almheiri/Dong/Harlow 2014) — The bulk geometry of quantum gravity is reconstructed by the boundary like an error-correcting code. Lose some boundary qubits → can still reconstruct bulk operators inside the entanglement wedge. This IS "memories protected by ECC all the way down" applied at cosmological scale.

Plus related precedents:

- **Jacobson 1995** — Derived Einstein equations from entanglement-entropy + Clausius (the precedent for the low-energy-limit step in the proof strategy below)
- **QBism** (Fuchs/Mermin/Schack) — Quantum mechanics as observer-relative probability assignments; maps onto "Pay Attention" as observer primitive
- **Causal sets / causal dynamical triangulation** (Sorkin, Loll) — Discrete causal order as fundamental; maps onto "Remember When" as temporal causal structure
- **Wheeler "It from Bit"** — All physical reality emerges from information-theoretic primitives
- **Carse's Finite and Infinite Games** — game-theoretic framework with no-end-state; maps onto why the universe doesn't approach equilibrium

## Proof strategy

Four-step research program:

1. **Formalize Remember-When + Pay-Attention as categorical primitives** — probably a topos with an internal monad for memory + an internal modal operator for attention (QBism's observer-relative basis maps onto the modal operator).

2. **Show the infinite-game extension produces a topos that has the algebraic structure of a quantum-error-correcting code** (HaPPY-like). The game-theoretic structure of "multiple players reconstructing shared state under noise" is structurally identical to "boundary observers reconstructing bulk operators under noise."

3. **Show the emergent geometry satisfies Einstein equations in a low-energy limit** — Jacobson's 1995 derivation is the precedent. If entanglement-entropy + game-theoretic action-principle → Clausius → Einstein equations works, the isomorphism is grounded.

4. **Predict ONE thing existing QG theories don't** (the falsifiability check). Without this, the work is at best a reformulation; with it, the work is a genuine new physical theory. Candidates:
   - Specific constraints on cosmological observers' coordination capabilities (multi-oracle quorum bounds → physical signature?)

   - Privacy/information-theoretic bounds on what observers can extract about the bulk
