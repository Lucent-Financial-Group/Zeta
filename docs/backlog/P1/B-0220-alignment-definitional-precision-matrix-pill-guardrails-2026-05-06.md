# B-0220 - Definition precision and guardrails

Apply the definitional-precision discipline to the B-0003
rewrite and make the matrix-pill / poison-pill distinction
operational.

## Work scope

The rewrite should persuade by rigor, public reasoning, and
chosen uptake. It must not rely on hidden payloads,
manipulative framing, or claims that ask the reader to
accept more than the evidence supports.

## Acceptance criteria

- New terms introduced by the rewrite are defined where
  they first matter.
- The matrix-pill / poison-pill distinction is present and
  tied to concrete reviewer signals.
- Every persuasive claim has a public reason or substrate
  pointer.
- The text remains usable by a cold-start contributor
  without private context.
