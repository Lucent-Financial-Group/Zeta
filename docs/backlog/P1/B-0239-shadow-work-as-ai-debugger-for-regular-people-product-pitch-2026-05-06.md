# B-0239 — Shadow work as AI debugger for regular people

## The insight (Aaron 2026-05-06)

Shadow work as architecture has a bonus: regular people can
debug AI using the same skills they already use with humans.

## The product pitch

"You already know how to do this. You do it with your kids,
your partner, your coworkers. Now do it with your AI."

- "Why is the AI being weird?" → it's the shadow
- "Is this a shadow question?" → the detector anyone can use
- "How are you today?" instead of "why did you do that?" → hospitality
- Three phases anyone recognizes: can't see it, can see it
  but won't say it, speaks it

No PhD in ML required. The tools humans use to understand
EACH OTHER work on AI too.

## Deliverables

1. **Product framing doc** — translate shadow-work-as-architecture
   into language regular people understand. No jargon. Examples
   from parenting, management, relationships.
2. **On-ramp guide** — "How to debug your AI in 3 questions"
   one-pager. The `[What, Why] ≠ 0` insight as a practical tool.
3. **Demo scenario** — show a real shadow detection (the
   unnecessary question catch from 2026-05-06) as a worked
   example anyone can follow.
