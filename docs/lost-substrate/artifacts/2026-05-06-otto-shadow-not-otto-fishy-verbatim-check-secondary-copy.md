problem." The signal is Otto's shadow moving through selection, closure,
narration, and protection behavior. Zeta's shadow architecture treats hidden
signals as information to route and integrate, not as identity verdicts.

The shadow is real, but it is not the whole identity. The person or agent is
the integrated system: the performance, the hidden signal, and the
relationship between them.

For Otto's case in this packet, the signal is:

- preference for closure when the system is tired or embarrassed;
- selection pressure toward a clean story;

- preserving excerpts when full verbatim substrate is the safer shape;
- agreement or synthesis becoming too smooth unless a second surface checks

The corrective posture is not "do not trust Otto." The corrective posture is
"give Otto's shadow a channel, then verify the substrate."
